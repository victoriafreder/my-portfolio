About Me

Welcome to my portfolio! I use my technical expertise and can-do attitude to satisfy client needs. Whether it's teaching, building websites, or inputting data, I make sure whoever I'm working with receives the quality of work that I myself would want to receive. I've learned HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Excel, Adobe Acrobat, and many other virtual tools so I can assist in several capacities as a contractor. I am a quick learner who enjoys solving problems and figuring out how things work. I have always had a knack for all things related to computers, often helping family members with their IT troubles. A couple friends encouraged me to look into web development (thanks, Javi and Jess!) and I was instantly fascinated with being able to create a website from scratch. I joined a boot camp where I became proficient at HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Git, and testing with Mocha. There were many challenges (and tears), but I wouldn't change a thing. Now that you've read my story, feel free to check out my experience and projects!
